Core Ingredients Revealed

Rosehip Oil

Rich in Vitamins A and C, promotes cell regeneration, reduces fine lines, brightens skin tone, and improves elasticity.

Shea Butter

Natural moisturizing factor, deeply hydrates dry skin, forms a protective barrier, and prevents moisture loss.

Jojoba Oil

Molecular structure similar to human sebum, easily absorbed, regulates oil production, and balances skin's moisture and oil.

Chamomile Extract

Exceptional anti-inflammatory and soothing properties, ideal for sensitive skin, relieves discomfort and redness.

Aloe Vera Gel

Natural humectant, soothes sun-exposed skin, promotes wound healing, and keeps skin smooth and hydrated.

Vitamin C

Potent antioxidant, inhibits melanin production, brightens skin, and prevents photoaging.
